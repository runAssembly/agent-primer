The agent-native company, in ten videos.

The fastest way to get the thesis we're building on: businesses run by AI agents, and the platform that makes that real. Watch top-to-bottom and you'll understand what an agent is, why this is bigger than SaaS, how agents actually work, and where Merkat fits. No reading required.

The one-line version

Software is shifting from tools people operate to agents that do the work. Every business will run a team of them. Merkat is where you build, run, and share that team — the shared workspace where a company's agents live and get smarter together.
